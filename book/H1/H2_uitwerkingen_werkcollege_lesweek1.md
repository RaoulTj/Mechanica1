## Opgave 1 (F2.2)

````{admonition} Antwoord
:class:  dropdown


\begin{align*}
|\overrightarrow{F_{res}}| = 665.75 \text{ N}
\end{align*}

\begin{align*}
\phi = 58.9 \text{ graden}
\end{align*}


```{admonition} Uitwerking
:class:  dropdown

Ontbind de kracht $\overrightarrow{F_1}$ in $F_{1;x}$ en $F_{1;y}$ :
\begin{align}
F_{1;x} &=  |\overrightarrow{F_1}| \cdot \cos (70) \\
& = 500 \cdot \cos (70) \\
& = 171.01 \text{ N}
\end{align}

\begin{align}
F_{1;y} &=  |\overrightarrow{F_1}| \cdot \sin (70) \\
& = 500 \cdot \sin (70) \\
& = 469.85 \text{ N}
\end{align}

Ontbind de kracht $\overrightarrow{F_2}$ in $F_{2;x}$ en $F_{2;y}$ :
\begin{align}
F_{2;x} &=  |\overrightarrow{F_2}| \cdot \cos (30) \\
& = 200 \cdot \cos (30) \\
& = 173.21 \text{ N}
\end{align}

\begin{align}
F_{2;y} &=  |\overrightarrow{F_2}| \cdot \sin (30) \\
& = 200 \cdot \sin (30) \\
& = 100 \text{ N}
\end{align}

Bepaal de resultante kracht $F_{res;x}$:
\begin{align}
F_{res;x} &=  F_{1;x} + F_{2;x} \\
& = 171.01 + 173.21 \\
& = 344.22 \text{ N}
\end{align}

Bepaal de resultante kracht $F_{res;y}$:
\begin{align}
F_{res;y} &=  F_{1;y} + F_{2;y} \\
& = 469.85 + 100 \\
& = 569.85 \text{ N}
\end{align}

Bepaal de grootte van de resultante kracht $\overrightarrow{F_{res}}$:
\begin{align}
|\overrightarrow{F_{res}}| &= \sqrt{(F_{res;x})^2 + (F_{res;y})^2 } \\
& = \sqrt{ (344.22)^2 + (569.85)^2 } \\
& = 665.75 \text{ N}
\end{align}

Bepaal de richting van de resultante kracht  $\overrightarrow{F_{res}}$:
\begin{align}
\tan (\phi) &= \dfrac{F_{res;y}}{F_{res;x}} \\
\phi &=  \tan^{-1} (\dfrac{F_{res;y}}{F_{res;x}}) \\
\phi &=  \tan^{-1} (\dfrac{ 569.85}{ 344.22}) \\
&= 58.9 \text{ graden}
\end{align}
```
````

## Opgave 2 (2.16)

````{admonition} Antwoord
:class:  dropdown


\begin{align*}
 \theta &= 54.9  \text{ graden}
\end{align*}

\begin{align*}
 |\overrightarrow{F_{res}}| &= 10.41 \text{ kN}
\end{align*}


```{admonition} Uitwerking
:class:  dropdown

Ontbind de kracht $\overrightarrow{F_{A}}$ in $F_{A;x}$ en $F_{A;y}$ :
\begin{align}
F_{A;x} &=  |\overrightarrow{F_{A}}| \cdot \sin (\phi) \\
& = 8 \cdot \sin (\theta)
\end{align}

\begin{align}
F_{A;y} &=  |\overrightarrow{F_{A}}| \cdot \cos (\phi) \\
& = 8 \cdot \cos (\theta)
\end{align}

Ontbind de kracht $\overrightarrow{F_{B}}$ in $F_{B;x}$ en $F_{B;y}$ :
\begin{align}
F_{B;x} &=  |\overrightarrow{F_{B}}| \cdot \sin (40) \\
& = 6 \cdot \sin (40) \\
& = 3.86 \text{ kN}
\end{align}

\begin{align}
F_{B;y} &=  |\overrightarrow{F_{B}}| \cdot \cos (40) \\
& = 6 \cdot \cos (40) \\
& = 4.60 \text{ kN}
\end{align}

De resultante van $\overrightarrow{F_{A}}$ en $\overrightarrow{F_{B}}$ is naar rechts gericht dus $F_{res;y} = 0$.

Bepaal de resultante kracht $F_{res;y}$:
\begin{align}
F_{res;y} &=  F_{A;y} + F_{B;y} \\
0 &= 8 \cdot \cos (\theta) - 4.60 \\
4.60 &= 8 \cdot \cos (\theta) \\
\cos (\theta) &= \dfrac{4.60}{8} \\
\theta &= \cos^{-1} (\dfrac{-4.60}{8}) \\
&= 54.9 \text{ graden}
\end{align}


Bepaal de resultante kracht $F_{res;x}$:
\begin{align}
F_{res;x} &=  F_{A;x} + F_{B;x} \\
& = 8 \cdot \sin (\phi) + 3.86 \\
& = 8 \cdot \sin (54.9) + 3.86 \\
& = 6.55 + 3.86 \\
& = 10.41 \text{ kN}
\end{align}

```
````

## Opgave 3 (F2.8)

````{admonition} Antwoord
:class:  dropdown


\begin{align*}
|\overrightarrow{F_{res}}| = 567.26 \text{ N}
\end{align*}

\begin{align*}
\phi = 38.1 \text{ graden}
\end{align*}



```{admonition} Uitwerking
:class:  dropdown

Ontbind de kracht $\overrightarrow{F_1}$ in $F_{1;x}$ en $F_{1;y}$ :
\begin{align}
F_{1;x} &=  |\overrightarrow{F_1}|  \\
& = 300 \text{ N}
\end{align}

\begin{align}
F_{1;y} &=  0\\
\end{align}

Ontbind de kracht $\overrightarrow{F_2}$ in $F_{2;x}$ en $F_{2;y}$ :
\begin{align}
F_{2;x} &=  |\overrightarrow{F_2}| \cdot \cos (30) \\
& = 400 \cdot \cos (30) \\
& = 346.41 \text{ N}
\end{align}

\begin{align}
F_{2;y} &=  |\overrightarrow{F_2}| \cdot \sin (30) \\
& = 400 \cdot \sin (30) \\
& = 200 \text{ N}
\end{align}

Ontbind de kracht $\overrightarrow{F_3}$ in $F_{3;x}$ en $F_{3;y}$ :
\begin{align}
\dfrac{F_3;x}{4} = \dfrac{|\overrightarrow{F_3}|}{5} \\
F_{3;x} &=  |\overrightarrow{F_3}| \cdot \dfrac{4}{5} \\
& = 250 \cdot \dfrac{4}{5} \\
& = 200 \text{ N}
\end{align}

\begin{align}
\dfrac{F_3;y}{3} = \dfrac{|\overrightarrow{F_3}|}{5} \\
F_{3;y} &=  |\overrightarrow{F_3}| \cdot \dfrac{3}{5} \\
& = 250 \cdot \dfrac{3}{5} \\
& = 150 \text{ N}
\end{align}

Bepaal de resultante kracht $F_{res;x}$:
\begin{align}
F_{res;x} &=  F_{1;x} + F_{2;x} - F_{3;x}\\
& = 300 + 346.41 - 200\\
& = 446.41 \text{ N}
\end{align}

Bepaal de resultante kracht $F_{res;y}$:
\begin{align}
F_{res;y} &=  F_{1;y} + F_{2;y} + F_{3;y} \\
& = 0 + 200 + 150 \\
& = 350 \text{ N}
\end{align}

Bepaal de grootte van de resultante kracht $\overrightarrow{F_{res}}$:
\begin{align}
|\overrightarrow{F_{res}}| &= \sqrt{(F_{res;x})^2 + (F_{res;y})^2 } \\
& = \sqrt{ (446.41)^2 + (350)^2 } \\
& = 567.26 \text{ N}
\end{align}

Bepaal de richting van de resultante kracht $\overrightarrow{F_{res}}$:
\begin{align}
\tan (\phi) & = \dfrac{F_{res;y}}{F_{res;x}} \\
\phi &=  \tan^{-1} (\dfrac{F_{res;y}}{F_{res;x}}) \\
\phi &=  \tan^{-1} (\dfrac{ 350}{ 446.41}) \\
&= 38.1 \text{ graden}
\end{align}
```
````

## Opgave 4 (F2.10)

````{admonition} Antwoord
:class:  dropdown


\begin{align*}
|\overrightarrow{F_{2}}| = 236.10 \text{ N}
\end{align*}

\begin{align*}
\theta =  31.76 \text{ graden}
\end{align*}


```{admonition} Uitwerking
:class:  dropdown

Ontbind de kracht $\overrightarrow{F_1}$ in $F_{1;x}$ en $F_{1;y}$ :
\begin{align}
F_{1;x} &=   |\overrightarrow{F_1}|  \cdot \cos (45) \\
& = 600 \cdot \cos (45) \\
& = 424.26 \text{ N}
\end{align}

\begin{align}
F_{1;y} &=   |\overrightarrow{F_1}|  \cdot \sin (45) \\
& = 600 \cdot \sin (45) \\
& = 424.26  \text{N}
\end{align}

Ontbind de kracht $\overrightarrow{F_2}$ in $F_{2;x}$ en $F_{2;y}$ :
\begin{align}
F_{2;x} &=  |\overrightarrow{F_2}| \cdot \cos (\theta) \\
& = F_2 \cdot \cos (\theta)
\end{align}

\begin{align}
F_{2;y} &=   |\overrightarrow{F_2}|  \cdot \sin (\theta) \\
& = F_2 \cdot \sin (\theta) \\
\end{align}

Ontbind de kracht $\overrightarrow{F_3}$ in $F_{3;x}$ en $F_{3;y}$ :
\begin{align}
\dfrac{F_3;x}{5} &= \dfrac{|\overrightarrow{F_3}| }{13} \\
F_{3;x} &=  |\overrightarrow{F_3}|  \cdot \dfrac{5}{13} \\
& = 325 \cdot \dfrac{5}{13} \\
& = 125 \text{ N}
\end{align}

\begin{align}
\dfrac{F_3;y}{12} &= \dfrac{|\overrightarrow{F_3}| }{13} \\
F_{3;y} &=  |\overrightarrow{F_3}|  \cdot \dfrac{12}{13} \\
& = 325 \cdot \dfrac{12}{13} \\
& = 300 \text{ N}
\end{align}

De resultante kracht $F_{res;x}=750$:
\begin{align}
F_{res;x} &=  F_{1;x} + F_{2;x} + F_{3;x} \\
750 &= 424.26 + |\overrightarrow{F_2}| \cdot \cos (\theta) + 125 \\
200.74 &= |\overrightarrow{F_2}| \cdot \cos (\theta) \\
|\overrightarrow{F_2}| &= \dfrac{200.74}{\cos (\theta)}
\end{align}

De resultante van $F_1$, $F_2$ en $F_3$ ligt op de x-as dus $F_{res;y} = 0$. \
Bepaal de resultante kracht $F_{res;y}$:
\begin{align}
F_{res;y} &=  F_{1;y} + F_{2;y} + F_{3;y} \\
0 &= -424.26 + |\overrightarrow{F_2}| \cdot \sin (\theta) + 300   \\
124.26 &= |\overrightarrow{F_2}| \cdot \sin (\theta)
\end{align}

Substitutie van $F_2$ geeft:
\begin{align}
124.26 &= |\overrightarrow{F_2}| \cdot \sin (\theta) \\
124.26 &= \dfrac{200.74}{\cos (\theta)} \cdot \sin (\theta) \\
124.26 &= 200.74 \cdot \dfrac{\sin (\theta)}{\cos (\theta)} \\
124.26 &= 200.74 \cdot \tan(\theta) \\
\tan(\theta) &= \dfrac{ 124.26}{200.74} \\
\theta &= \tan^{-1} (\dfrac{ 124.26}{200.74}) \\
&= 31.76 \text{ graden}
\end{align}

$\theta$ invullen geeft de grootte van F:
\begin{align}
124.26 &= |\overrightarrow{F_2}| \cdot \sin (\theta) \\
124.26 &= |\overrightarrow{F_2}| \cdot \sin (31.76) \\
|\overrightarrow{F_2}| &= \dfrac{124.26}{\sin (31.76) } \\
&= 236.10 \text{ N}
\end{align}
```
````
